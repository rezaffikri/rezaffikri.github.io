Summary

ObonBoy is a Slack bot designed for daily stand-ups and weekly work reporting. ObonBoy initiates individual chats with each employee to facilitate daily stand-ups, providing reminders. Once employees submit their updates, a summary of all daily stand-ups is visible in a designated channel. Additionally, every week, ObonBoy engages each employee to collect their weekly work reports, neatly formatted in the sequence and structure specified by the Engineering Group Head, built with C# (.Net Core).
